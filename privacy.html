Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

MyAgentTee ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you use our website, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information we collect

  • Order and account data: email, shipping address, and order details you enter at checkout.
  • Payment data: processed by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.
  • Optional fields: such as an "agent name" label you choose to attach to an order.
  • Technical data: basic server logs may include IP address, browser type, and timestamps for security and operations.

2. How we use information

We use information to:

  • fulfill and ship orders;
  • communicate about your order (e.g., confirmations, support);
  • detect fraud, abuse, or technical issues;
  • comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing

We share data with service providers needed to run the store, such as payment processing (Stripe) and shipping/fulfillment partners you implicitly use when we ship your order. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Retention

We keep order records as long as needed for accounting, taxes, support, and legal compliance, then delete or anonymize where appropriate.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal information. Contact us at hello@myagenttee.com to make a request. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

6. Children

Our site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. International users

If you order from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the U.S. or other locations where our providers operate.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will change. Material changes may be announced on the site or by email where appropriate.

9. Contact

Questions: hello@myagenttee.com
