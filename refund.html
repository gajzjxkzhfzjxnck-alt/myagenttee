Refund & Exchange Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

We want you to be happy with MyAgentTee. This policy explains returns, exchanges, and defective items for our fixed-design commemorative t-shirts.

1. Fixed-design products

Our tee uses a fixed graphic and standard sizes. Please review the size guide and product page before ordering. Because items may be produced on demand, eligibility for returns may be limited as described below.

2. Defective or wrong items

If you receive the wrong item, a misprinted product, or a clearly defective item, contact us within 14 days of delivery at hello@myagenttee.com with your order number and photos. We will work with you on a replacement or refund, as appropriate.

3. Size and fit

If the item matches what you ordered but does not fit, exchanges may be offered only when inventory allows and within a stated window. Shipping costs for exchanges may apply. Contact us with your order details.

4. Change of mind

Because apparel may be produced for your order, we may not accept returns for change of mind once production has started. If your order has not yet entered production, we may cancel and refund at our discretion—email us as soon as possible after purchase.

5. Refund method

Approved refunds are generally returned to the original payment method via our payment provider (Stripe). Timing may depend on your bank or card issuer.

6. Shipping costs

Original shipping charges may be non-refundable unless the return is due to our error. Return shipping for non-defective items may be your responsibility unless we specify otherwise in writing.

7. How to contact us

Email hello@myagenttee.com with your order number, a short description of the issue, and any photos.
